### Family resettlement

German Chancellor Angela Merkelâ€™s Cabinet voted to resettle an additional 1,000 migrants per month, provided they are the direct relatives of refugees already living in Germany\. Subsidiary protection is an asylum status which is only valid for one year and can be renewed afterwards for another two years\. In general, there are a lot of requirements for the family reunification process through an embassy\.
