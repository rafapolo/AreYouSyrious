### NGO Registry Violates European Convention on Human Rights, Council of Europe Says

The Council of Europe said the Greek Ministry of Immigration Policy‚Äôs plan to register all NGOs and NGO workers would [violate human rights](https://www.efsyn.gr/ellada/dikaiomata/250544_katapeltis-symboylio-tis-eyropis-gia-mitroo-mko-toy-mitaraki) \.

The process of registration would put undue cost and time burdens on NGOs, especially for small organizations\. The requirements for registration are very strict, require disclosure of very personal information, and applications have been arbitrarily rejected by authorities, all of which would greatly impede civil society‚Äôs ability to function\. Thus, it violates the right to privacy and the right to assemble freely, which are both protected in the European Convention on Human Rights\.

Hopefully the EU‚Äôs action in this case can help NGOs in Greece continue their vital work in an increasingly hostile environment\.

More people in distress in Greek waters [contacted AlarmPhone](https://twitter.com/alarm_phone/status/1279143968043925504) on Friday night\. Water was entering their boat and they needed to be rescued urgently\. AlarmPhone alerted the Greek Coast Guard and a boat was on the scene, but they did not conduct a rescue\. AlarmPhone unfortunately lost contact with the boat in distress and they fear the worst\. This inaction, especially a few days after 4 people died in the Aegean, is unacceptable\!

Rising temperatures are affecting people on the move across Greece\. However, the police started attacking and arresting them whenever they went to get water, even though nobody else is using the pipe or the field\. Denying people access to water, especially during the hot Greek summer, is a violation of human rights\.

The situation in Greece is taking an unimaginable toll on the mental health of people on the move\. A 45\-year\-old Afghan man [died by suicide](https://www.efsyn.gr/ellada/astynomiko/250392_apaghonismos-prosfyga-se-domi-sta-oinofyta) in a camp in Viotia\. The man had previously experienced mental health issues and the prospect of deportation back to a war\-torn country probably seemed too much\. Several other people living in Greek camps have attempted or died by suicide recently\. The instability and degrading nature of the Greek asylum system is causing irreparable emotional damage to people and is needlessly cruel\.

The Greek government‚Äôs scheme to expel registered refugees from camps is causing unimaginable levels of homelessness and distress\. In Victoria Square in Athens alone, [240 people](https://www.facebook.com/Donate4Refugees.org/photos/a.1660159314274382/2681541805469456/?type=1&theater) are camping with no access to toilet facilities and running water\. This is a tragedy that could easily be avoided, as Greece receives EU funding to provide safe housing for people on the move\.

An [official investigation](https://astraparis.gr/ta-eyrimata-deichnoyn-emprismo-tis-apothikis-apopeira-kai-gia-aytokinito/?fbclid=IwAR3dt-Dle6FTvkcpQ3rclQLw1PSaJResu9bJjDkHVVceXRVIRYK3X1wcJbs) found that the warehouse fire in Chios in March was caused by arson\. However, the police [have not found](https://twitter.com/VassilisTsarnas/status/1278990419523784705?fbclid=IwAR0QxVqnyUxqjrN5KBQDnj0FKf_x0A02Q1AnlQUz2Kv75VSZzKXiM6GQYRQ) the perpetrators even after four months\.

### State of Emergency on Ocean Viking

After days stuck at sea amid deteriorating conditions, the Ocean Viking declared a [state of emergency](https://sosmediterranee.com/press/state-of-emergency-declared-on-the-ocean-viking-immediate-disembarkation-required/?fbclid=IwAR0S8xFBzsDJF1AO0vnleen8-5M8X6DmInT5iANLa-RJiF6p9MsTZy4STJ8) and called for immediate disembarkation\. There are [over 180 people](https://www.infomigrants.net/en/post/25790/waiting-game-continues-on-board-ocean-viking?fbclid=IwAR0QxVqnyUxqjrN5KBQDnj0FKf_x0A02Q1AnlQUz2Kv75VSZzKXiM6GQYRQ) on board in overcrowded conditions\. Some survivors have been on board since June 25th\.

Declaring a state of emergency is unprecedented, but the crew feel that they are unable to guarantee the safety of everyone on board\. In the past 24 hours, six people have attempted suicide\. Many others have expressed suicidal thoughts and other signs of mental distress\. The people on board started a [hunger strike](https://www.faz.net/aktuell/politik/ausland/nach-suizid-versuchen-rettungsschiff-ocean-viking-ruft-notstand-aus-16845177.html?fbclid=IwAR3m-ymmo2WCab-wXntFz3A9L-VjlRID-CyWlgQVmzKU7DSisjyYtqODHR0) in protest of their treatment\.

In the past week, Ocean Viking sent seven requests for a Place of Safety, all of which were ignored or denied outright\. They also requested a transfer just of the 44 survivors suffering the most mental distress, but even that was denied\. As [MEP Erik Marquardt explains](https://twitter.com/ErikMarquardt/status/1279134484785397765) , this callous treatment of people in distress is due to racism, pure and simple\.

Instead of providing any meaningful aid, Italy sent‚Äîa [psychiatrist](https://www.facebook.com/sosmedsuisse/posts/1435094286682706?hc_location=ufi) \. No amount of talk therapy or antidepressants can improve the mental health situation of people that have suffered unspeakable trauma and have now been stuck on a ship for over a week in worsening weather conditions\. The solution is simple‚Äîprovide a safe port for the people on board to disembark and receive the physical and mental health treatment they need and a safe place to rebuild their lives\.

Otherwise, there will be more blood on Europe‚Äôs hands\.

ITALY
### Will Italy Be Prosecuted for its Role in the Easter Tragedy?

Following the example of their Maltese counterparts, a group of Italian human rights advocates [asked Italian prosecutors](https://www.infomigrants.net/en/post/25795/human-rights-groups-ask-prosecutors-to-determine-if-italy-is-complicit-in-easter-tragedy-for-migrant-boat?preview=1593782541366&fbclid=IwAR0h3XiqO63IT3lcv2UNeiz_aGtJUFKB5qN_4HwVeF5UUkA1JkzT8QYAppA) to investigate their government‚Äôs role in the Easter Tragedy\.

The Easter Tragedy refers to an incident that unfolded over Easter weekend, where AlarmPhone received word of a ship in distress in the Mediterranean\. The NGO alerted the Maltese authorities, because the ship was in Maltese SAR, and the Italians, because it was only a few kilometers off the coast of Lampedusa\. The authorities did nothing, and the people on board were only ‚Äúrescued‚Äù and taken back to Libya \(against their will\) four days later\. In the meantime, 12 people died\.

Even though the ship was in Maltese SAR, Italy could have easily rescued those on board, but made no effort to do so\.

Since June 1st, [over 80,000 applications](https://www.infomigrants.net/en/post/25791/more-than-80-000-applications-for-migrant-regularization-in-italy?fbclid=IwAR1LoKMiPAxmkyyI7w0LzLRuCKnhR4Ncue74u_1Qaso_JUAXcUomrLm78eU) for regularization of immigration status have been submitted in Italy\. The vast majority of these applications came from domestic care workers\.

DENMARK
### Tunisian Activist Faces Deportation Despite Danger to His Life

Nacer Amari, a vocal atheist and human rights activist, [faces deportation](https://humanists.international/2020/07/tunisian-activist-faces-imminent-deportation-from-denmark-posing-an-extreme-risk-to-his-well-being/?fbclid=IwAR2On9Ku5vpX8vD-4JeczMV01mpAlrFxbfJW2irb6SLE1ptBgdBXlERbx0U) to his home country of Tunisia\. The Danish Refugee Appeals Board says Amari cannot claim asylum on the basis of his atheism and human rights activities because he will not face any issues if he returns to Tunisia\. Amari fled his home country in 2012 after receiving death threats, and after initially settling in Romania had to flee from there as well\.

Not only is Tunisia in general a hostile environment for dissidents, according to research conducted by Humanists International, Nacer Amari clearly faces credible threat as an individual\. Sadly, this is just the latest instance of the Danish authorities rejecting asylum applications from people fleeing danger\.
