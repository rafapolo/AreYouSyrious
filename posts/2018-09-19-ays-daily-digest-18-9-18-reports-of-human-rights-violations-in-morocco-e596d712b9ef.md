On the mainland, it is [reported](https://twitter.com/mariamjxde/status/1042061533621174272) that people have occupied the building of Solidarity Now in Larissa and are demanding the end of the Skype pre\-registration system, as well as highlighting other problems in the Greek asylum system\. For people who arrived in Greece by land, the only way to apply for asylum is to call a Skype hotline and ask for an appointment, or â€œpre\-register\.â€ This hotline is hardly available and extremely difficult to access; it can take months to get through and book an appointment, especially for non\-Arabic speakers, who may only have one two\-hour slot per week in which they can call\. And for the period of weeks or months in which refugees are unable to get through to Skype, they are considered illegally present in Greece and cannot access key medical services or apply for official accommodation\.

The Foodkind team in Athens is asking for volunteers to help in the Oinofyta camp, some 60km north of Athens\. Most support is needed in the kitchen, but the activities also include games, sports and activities with children\. Housing is available\. For this as well as for transportation costs, the team asks for a small contribution from interested volunteers\. If you are interested, write an E\-Mail to action@foodKIND\.org\. The [Feox rescue team](https://www.facebook.com/1584119558539350/photos/a.1659925364292102/2217112328573400/?type=3) on Chios is asking for more people in October and November, too\.

Campfire Innovation is [extending an invite](https://www.facebook.com/events/507305026409303/) to a Smart Aid Gathering in Athens on Thursday, 27th September\. The participants will discuss how to provide dignified aid efficiently\. Hestia Hellas, I am you, and Solomon will be part of the panel\.
### Italy

In its weekly snapshot, the [UNHCR](https://data2.unhcr.org/en/documents/details/65775) recorded 616 new arrivals to Italy for the week ending on September 16th\.
