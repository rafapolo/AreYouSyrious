ITALY
#### More people arriving from Tunisia than from Libya

Arrivals in Italy are on the rise, around 15,000 since the beginning of the year, but still very far from being any kind of emergency for a G7 country with a population of 60 million\. Instead the Italian right is stirring its followers and blaming people on the move for new coronavirus spikes and comparing them to tourists on cruises\. In 2020, more people on the move have arrived in Italy from Tunisia than from Libya\. The Tunisian economy has plummeted in the last year, with the former president warning the parliament that most public companies were in bankruptcy and the COVID\-19 pandemic has only worsened the situation, especially hitting migrant workers, who are resorting to leaving for Europe\. A series of policies that were exactly intended to drastically and criminally reduce the number of arrivals from Libya\.
