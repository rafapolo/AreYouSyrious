#### WORTH READING
- Word matter\. This is why in AYS we try to shape the narrative differently, use different language that we believe fits the reality of the situation and core of what is in place much better\. Much of the language widely used in the context of migration and human rights when it comes to people fleeing their homes, is based on the political terminology shaped to stress the securitsation and border regimes rather than freedom and right to movement\. ERIM is a project that brings such terminology to the fore\. Latest in their glossary is the often used termin ‚Äî irregular migration\.
