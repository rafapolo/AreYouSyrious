With all this information about Frontex, how can we comfortably celebrate European values, knowing what they are? What would we really be celebrating? Hypocrisy, violence at the border, [violations of privacy](https://www.statewatch.org/news/2021/may/europol-should-step-up-data-exchange-on-civil-unrest-council-presidency-proposes/?fbclid=IwAR2ksjzX1OsyqumtuMpPRWhqyYDAHaRaRzAiu4ti18UdpYDWtBPsUECndDY) , and racism, just to name a few\. We will celebrate ‚Äúpeace and unity‚Äù when that includes everyone, not just people lucky enough to be born in the right countries with the right skin color and the right passports\.

#### SEA
#### Multiple boats in distress in Mediterranean

Over the weekend, AlarmPhone was contacted by a boat with [55 people in distress](https://twitter.com/alarm_phone/status/1391383482727870464?fbclid=IwAR2aRhAczrBRb18LcNn1nWQ5C0AIiSx_oD9h9NGLaZaXJeHBR7FT0DZBG-U) , by another one with [80 people on board](https://twitter.com/alarm_phone/status/1391357172228005891?fbclid=IwAR18vRUllhAj13KsKwvYGBDniNNKOQbXC4GAKeC_9TaBq55Y_qvLJgTTqSw) , including a baby that was just 2 months old, and by a third carrying [96 people](https://twitter.com/alarm_phone/status/1391355516463550464?fbclid=IwAR1qhk3ukXnwTErVbJS85I_6aOKrh4TQ5IuEckdETq0ESmPsGYbPibIQqMA) \. All three were in the Maltese SAR zone\. The Armed Forces of Malta did not respond to requests for help from AlarmPhone or to requests for [comment by journalists](https://timesofmalta.com/articles/view/newborn-among-80-migrants-on-leaking-boat-in-maltas-sar.870601?fbclid=IwAR1cXT5i_ECRzF1lpkqX7RN0XGiCdCfw5gF4FMJONrQNialcMRCrl316Aoc) \. These people are in grave danger\. Many are ill after spending several days at sea without food or water\. One of the boats is leaking while another is running out of fuel\.

There were also several distress cases near Lampedusa\. AlarmPhone received a call from a boat [carrying 97 people](https://twitter.com/alarm_phone/status/1391384252961460224?fbclid=IwAR1jYpniVxo1xyaWzC1NejFIbGbwTzERz4wumpEGdfpYOEvnTTS7MnJhHmM) \. Overall, Lampedusa saw [about a thousand arrivals](https://twitter.com/AngiKappa/status/1391341631874420736?fbclid=IwAR2v1M7e_exlIxGri-hjmKRl1AVuotTC2n5I0aYoovn6GHFRvxbXOS-gaYs) in 24 hours, arriving in [nine boats](https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/four-boats-carrying-hundred-migrants-land-italys-lampedusa-report-2021-05-09/?rpc=401&fbclid=IwAR0UghKsTTna6Ndmy9T1-LqFkiG-EeSdwdOI4hM8BKKmCo9-x-Cph-UkLRw) \.

In the Western Mediterranean, [16 people](https://twitter.com/maydayterraneo/status/1391342783928090631?fbclid=IwAR1ryw8fEb9RJEqDf3-vv8e60ZRFkwE6nWb88qc3PkAoY5ndqeqnM6TuWEk) were rescued off the coast of Cartagena\.

Included in the hundreds of people in distress on the Mediterranean this weekend are the [140 people](https://twitter.com/msehlisafa/status/1391360354333216768?fbclid=IwAR22GVUCP_hN7mmg0adTvvyHlldwgaBzh6XqV3TiLxHKCVNJJ1MsT8S2uzs) that were intercepted and pulled back to Libya, where they will face torture and awful conditions\.

While rescue NGOs, including the new ship the [Sea Eye 4](https://twitter.com/seaeyeorg/status/1391060331087138819?fbclid=IwAR04-Ffw50zmr8GfcblkTn74CeJNNE4hI7v3Ca5NFuKMGXTxrhjK8UABVeE) which just left its port and the [Aita Mari](https://twitter.com/Seebruecke_intl/status/1391090999309524992?fbclid=IwAR3Ouq89ey5InT65WXmGZetjsuSL0l2WchiZlAl1N4PQPOmsuwKysEXrpNE) which is returning to the Mediterranean, do their best to help as many people as they can, they cannot save everyone without the cooperation of national authorities\. They especially cannot help people when they are tied up in administrative detention, which is what happened to the [Alan Kurdi](https://twitter.com/gorden_isler/status/1391320845574938625?fbclid=IwAR1-mekrxEKXIeYveDqGJAy-CCb3MUD8ocqMlDPVKtYqmkM60nGo66TF7Qw) for six months\.

For context on how this extraordinary weekend at sea fits into the overall patterns of people moving and European negligence, read SOS Mediterranee‚Äôs [2020 report](https://sosmediterranee.de/press/presse-pflicht-zur-seenotrettung-gilt-auch-in-der-pandemie-jahresbericht-2020/?fbclid=IwAR2fb7pwoIWGo2cYZ7WgZiRI0mBe9LDJNZjIQ5TWNs9qIjIlhdaMLwFyU20) \.
#### GREECE
#### Funeral of Abdul Jaleel, man who died in Chios

This weekend was [the funeral](https://twitter.com/Theurgia_Goetia/status/1390989135113035778) of Abdul Jaleel, a young father who died in the camp in Chios\. In this [horrific case](https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2021/may/07/a-scene-out-of-the-middle-ages-dead-refugee-found-surrounded-by-rats-at-greek-camp?fbclid=IwAR1uwY7SY3uYUNTg2oqxzrFX0AQbdvg2nKdjgdeDnV3-RLhF53goDM_rnRM) , his body was found surrounded by rats, exposing the conditions that people are forced to live in\.

Rest in peace Mr\. Jaleel\. No human should ever have to live or die like this\.

[Another body](https://twitter.com/lk2015r/status/1390778113009803266?fbclid=IwAR0h9aKB-UsVkI7bCndnqPupipeAPy_eXNPAAWcSyB64n_BjpMV3fNKsZG0) was found in Evros\. In the past month and a half, four people have died in the region trying to cross the border\.

[Horrifying racial abuse](https://twitter.com/VassilisTsarnas/status/1391435395959820295?fbclid=IwAR1Sri5AMIURC7ta4Qa7kEdDgBSQLNqwkQ1bPCtu6XTVdI539S7cQiMLrsM) on a bus in Athens: a bus driver and passengers forced a man to give up his seat because he is a foreigner\. The man simply asked for people to respect social distancing measures and then received a torrent of racial abuse\.

Parwana Amiri, whose words we share frequently, is one of thousands of young Afghans stuck in the Greek camps\. If you are not familiar with her story or the situation in Ritsona camp, read a profile [here](https://www.opendemocracy.net/en/how-a-young-afghan-woman-trapped-at-europes-borders-found-her-voice/?fbclid=IwAR2S6OBZ-AZc001qZgji3RI2Vd_yD3yHNJrUO5jWw8-3yrWvwooPCJBrjWw) \.

#### FRANCE
#### More Calais evictions

On Saturday morning, French authorities conducted evictions in [five different places\.](https://twitter.com/HumanRightsObs/status/1391091792179777540?fbclid=IwAR310jZwYJaDKeqo2pYTOBkAS9HYjNtLvirFFegQVanoSBLFHJ7WSo3y-d4) They seized 70 tents and 15 tarpaulins\. Like during previous evictions, the people will probably be back soon because they have nowhere to go, except now they will not have any shelter\.

The busy French authorities also conducted [discriminatory ID checks](https://twitter.com/HumanRightsObs/status/1391071891914436615?fbclid=IwAR1F80u0Z-maybPyG1a49MlBX2l7HQci76geI8XCedx6Xqeppv9DqJQZl_4) in the Calais train station, targeting people of color\.

Amnesty International conducted a survey of people in Calais on their attitudes towards people on the move and volunteers\. You can find the results in [this article](https://www.lavoixdunord.fr/998763/article/2021-05-07/que-pensent-les-calaisiens-de-la-presence-des-migrants-et-de-leurs-aidants?&pwback&fbclid=IwAR3ESdDDAKYqlnEXP-jwV2B_x4WztQMnvoAD-DxY0tXGvqrU5-MXjgsguC0) \(paywalled\) \.
#### UK
#### New Asylum plan receives criticism from European countries, UN

The UNHCR sharply criticized the Home Office‚Äôs post\-Brexit migration plans, calling them a threat to [Britain‚Äôs ‚Äúglobal credibility\.‚Äù](https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2021/may/09/eu-countries-snub-priti-patels-plans-to-return-asylum-seekers?fbclid=IwAR2K7VLyVLm7xchk_QID1Wd8S-8ddZwc8pQ4Fwheajg_3BOg2CjBobuouTc) The plans, which include proposals like deporting people who‚Äôve transited through so\-called safe third countries, treating asylum applicants differently based on the method of arrival, and other violations of human rights, go against international laws on refugees\. A representative of the UNHCR, Rosella Pagliuchi\-Lor, said that the plan threatened to undermine the UN‚Äôs refugee convention and threaten international relations\.

The plans are also impossible to implement, as not a single European government has agreed to take people deported from the UK\. What will likely happen is that people will be imprisoned in administrative detention without the possibility to apply for asylum or leave their situation\.
