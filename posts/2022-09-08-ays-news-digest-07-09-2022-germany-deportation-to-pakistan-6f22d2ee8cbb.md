#### Children have died at sea

The sad news of a four year old who died at sea has been published\. Activist group Civil Fleet reported on the news and gave [a full account of what happened](https://thecivilfleet.wordpress.com/2022/09/07/four-year-old-girl-dies-after-greece-and-malta-failed-to-launch-rescue-three-days-after-activists-raised-the-alarm/) \. After days at sea, the child was finally [flown by Navy helicopter](https://flashnews.gr/post/553672/tragodia-me-4chrono-paidi-pou-diekomisthi-choris-tis-aisthiseis-tou-sto-nosokomeio-chanion/?fbclid=IwAR3W4A5IjB9SoyxP6tCJal6blDLz1VZblCDqp9JwR3Hl0VF7RCdX584uK6Q) to hospital in Greece after the intervention of a cargo ship\.
