The EU‚Äôs [temporary protection Directive](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32001L0055) does not afford third country nationals [who have moved to Ukraine in recent years](http://eulawanalysis.blogspot.com/2022/02/temporary-protection-for-ukrainians-in.html) the same protections as Ukrainian citizen nationals, noting that temporary protection is afford to ‚Äòpersons leaving their country or region of origin‚Äô\. Over 470,000 third country nationals in Ukraine, including many overseas students and labour migrants, are currently stranded, trying to find safe passage to Moldova, Slovakia, Hungary, and Poland\.
